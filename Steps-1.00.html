PROBLEM STATEMENT 1
The year 2002 marked the 30th anniversary of the passage of Title IX, which prohibits discrimination by gender in any federally funded educational activity. After 30 years, athletic opportunities for female undergraduates had significantly expanded. For example, female share of college resources had risen to 42% in 2002 from only 15% in 1972.
 
In 2007 the ratios for male teams to female teams were as follows: 
  •  Student aid – 1.65
  •  Recruiting Expense – 2.71
  •  Salary – 1.40
  •  Operating Expense – 3.54
